The Creative Capital - est. 1636

Timeline of Providence

  • 1636 - Roger Williams founds Providence
  • 1753 - Providence Library Company organized
  • 1762 - Statehouse built
  • 1815 - The Great Gale of 1815 causes extensive damage and flooding
  • 1872 - Construction begins on the Brown and Sharpe Manufacturing Company Complex along the Woonasquatucket River
  • 1975 - Buddy Cianci becomes mayor
  • 2013 - The landmark Industrial Trust Building, aka "Superman Building," loses its sole tenant, and goes dark
  • 2017 - The Silicon Rhode is paved!
